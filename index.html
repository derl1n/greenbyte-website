Features

Everything you need to understand and reduce your digital carbon footprint.

The problem

Modern internet use (streaming, cloud AI and large models) has a measurable carbon cost. Individuals rarely see this cost and therefore cannot change behaviour effectively.

Our solution

GreenByte estimates your digital CO₂ locally, translates it into relatable equivalents, gives AI-driven recommendations, and motivates behaviour change through achievements and leaderboards.

Real‑time tracking

Precise, local estimates of CO₂ for browsing, streaming and AI usage; no history leaves your device unless you opt in.

AI assistant

Contextual tips: e.g., switch to audio-only, download playlists, or schedule heavy jobs at low-carbon hours.

Achievements & leaderboards

Earn badges, compare with classmates, and unlock tasks that reduce your footprint. Both local and global leaderboards supported.

Privacy & control

Local-first design. You control uploads. Only aggregated anonymized metrics are shared when enabled.

Achievements & Leaderboards

Gamify your green habits and compete with the world.

Badges

Earn themed badges for behaviour: "Low Streamer", "Night Scheduler", "Cloud Minimalist". Badges show on your profile and leaderboard.

Leaderboards

Compare weekly and all-time scores with classmates, schools, or globally. Privacy safeguards ensure only aggregated ranks are visible.

GreenTokens

Convert saved CO₂ into GreenTokens redeemable for partner actions like tree planting or school projects.
